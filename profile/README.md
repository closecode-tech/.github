## 🌟 Our Platforms

### 1. PitchMap
*Interactive AI-Powered Sales Simulator*  
Practice real-world sales scenarios, receive instant AI feedback, and level up with gamified learning—ideal for onboarding, upskilling, and sales team readiness.

**Features:**
- Real-time AI sales simulations (calls, demos, objections)
- Instant feedback on tone, structure, and responses
- Gamified missions, XP, badges, and skills tracking
- Persona-based scenarios and objection handling drills
- CRM simulation and cold email builder
- Dashboards and analytics for individuals and teams

### 2. OpenRelay
*AI-Driven Tech Media Platform*  
Curated, AI-generated news, event coverage, and insightful content for the tech community, startups, and developers.

**Features:**
- Real-time AI news aggregation and curation
- Personalized feeds and event tracking
- Community-sourced insights and expert Q&A
- AI-generated explainers, digests, and technical deep-dives

### 3. Scale Plus
*Startup Enablement & Resource Platform*  
A digital hub offering curated resources, workspace tools, mentorship, and networking for founders, SMBs, and innovation hubs.

**Features:**
- Curated database of tools, funding, and resources
- Workspace and collaboration management
- Access to vetted mentors and advisors
- Community forums, events, and masterclasses

### 4. Retenify
*AI-Powered Customer Retention Platform*  
Predict churn, segment customers, automate retention campaigns, and boost lifetime value with actionable analytics.

**Features:**
- Churn prediction and risk scoring
- Automated, personalized retention campaigns
- Segmentation and cohort analysis
- Real-time analytics and dashboards
- CRM and support tool integrations
